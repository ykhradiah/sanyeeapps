What is a Baclk soldier Fly?
Hermetia illucens well known as the black soldier fly, is the leading insect species used in the animal feed and pet food sector. Native from south America this species naturalized most of the world for 400 years. This friendly species is not corps and can be found all around the world,but are happier under topics where we operate and carefully listen to their willing.



Replacing Fish Meal With Insect Meal in the diet of Atlantic salmon
Black soldier fly larvae meal is a great sustainable alternative to fish meal,that not only mantains performance, but also provides additional functional benefits. This interesting article shows evidence of new benefits from the use of BSF in Atlantic salmon diet. Arsenic levels in salmon seems to drop substantially with the increase of BSF larvae meal inclusion in their diet.


The Future of Sustainable Animal Feed and Pet Food.
The global animal feed industry is the largest and most important component of livestock raising,and fish and poultry farming. It is crucial to the industry to be able to provide animal protiens that are safe, plentiful and affordable. However, this industry is facing many issues,sustainability and the rapid growth of the meat,dairy and fish industry being the main ones.
